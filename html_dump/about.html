About Us

YourOpenDate started with an unlikely partnership between DJs and Computer Scientists in order to simplify bookings in the wedding and private event industry. YourOpenDate is designed to be a space where wedding couples and corporate clients alike can see all available dates for their favorite venues and vendors, all in one place.

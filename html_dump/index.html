Frequently Asked Questions


How Does the Posting Limit Work?
The posting limit allows no more than the limited number of Open Dates to be within the same month. For example, if you have the Plus Membership, you cannot have more than 5 Open Dates listed in June of next year.
The posting limit does not affect when or how often you can submit, modify, and delete your Open Dates from the website.

What is Your Open Date?
Your Open Date is a web-based software that allows you to share your available dates with wedding and event clients in your market.

How Does Your Open Date Work?
Wedding and Event clients come to YourOpenDate in search of venues and vendors for their date. Venues and Vendors post their availability to the site. As clients see venues and vendors available, they reach out to you directly.

Why Are There Three Different Prices?
Businesses come in many shapes and sizes, and we want to offer tools to businesses that fit their specific scale and needs.

What Is The Custom Calendar Link Feature and Why is it so Important?
The custom calendar link is a specially created URL that links directly to specifically your availability, filtering out all other venues and vendors. This in turn becomes a dashboard for prospective clients to view that shows every available date that you have, making availability and bookings much simpler.
